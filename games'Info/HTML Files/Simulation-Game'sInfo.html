A simulation video game is a super-category of video games designed to closely simulate real world activities.
Well-known examples are war games, business games, and role play simulation.
Simulation games can be used for various purposes such as training, analysis, prediction, or simply entertainment.






  •  Microsoft Flight Simulator

      Developed by :- Asobo Studio,Microsoft Corporation
      Published by :- Microsoft Corporation, Aerosoft, Xbox Game Studios
      IGN Ratings :- 10/10

      Microsoft Flight Simulator is one of the longest-running, best-known, iconic, and most comprehensive home   flight simulator programs on the market. It was an early product in the Microsoft application portfolio and   differed significantly from Microsoft's other software,which was largely business-oriented.

      Available for Platforms :- Windows,Xbox



  •  The Sims Simulator Series

      Developed by :- Electronic Arts, Maxis, The Sims Studio
      Published by :- Electronic Arts, Aspyr, EA Mobile, ak tronic Software & Services GmbH, Edge of Reality
      IGN Ratings :- 9.2/10

      The Sims series is one of the best-selling video game series of all time. The player creates virtual people   called "Sims", places them in houses, and helps direct their moods. Each successive expansion pack and game   in the series augmented what the player could do with their Sims.

      Available for Platforms :- Windows,Playstation,Xbox



  •  Cooking Simulator

      Developed by :- Big Cheese Studio, GameBoom VR, Forever Entertainment S. A.
      Published by :- Big Cheese Studio, PlayWay, Forever Entertainment S. A., CodeLQ Students
      IGN Ratings :- 9/10

      Cooking Simulator is a simulation cooking game developed by Polish team Big Cheese Studio. A mobile port,   titled Cooking Simulator Mobile, launched on Android devices on 20 October 2020.   •  Farming Simulator Series

      Developed by :- GIANTS Software
      Published by :- GIANTS Software,Focus Entertainment
      IGN Ratings :- 8/10
