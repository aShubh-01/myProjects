Strategy Games



Home Page Action-Adventure games Puzzle games Racing games Simulation games Strategy games

Strategy games are video games with a primary focus on high-level strategy, logistics and resource management.
They are usually divided into two main sub-categories: turn-based and real-time.
There are also many strategy cross/sub-genres that feature additional elements such as tactics, diplomacy, economics and exploration.






  •  Humans : Fall Flat

      Developed by :- No Brakes Games, Codeglue
      Published by :- Curve Games, 505 Games, MP Digital, LLC, Super Rare Games
      IGN Ratings :- 8/10

      Human: Fall Flat is a puzzle-platform game. It was initially released for Microsoft Windows, macOS,
      and Linux in July 2016. Reviewers praised the replayability of the puzzles and comedic animations.The game   has sold more than 30 million copies as of July 2021.

      Available for Platforms :- Windows,Playstation,Nintendo,IOS,Android,Mac,Linuz,Xbox


  •  Civilization Series

      Developed by :- Sid Meier, Firaxis Games, Aspyr, Activision
      Published by :- Firaxis Games, 2K Games, Electronic Arts, Aspyr
      IGN Ratings :- 10/10

      Civilization is a series of turn-based strategy video games, first released in 1991. There are six main   games in the series, as well as a number of expansion packs and spin-off games. The series is formative   example of the 4X genre.

      Available for Platforms :- Windows,DOS,Mac



  •  League of Legends

      Developed by :- Riot Games
      Published by :- Riot Games
      IGN Ratings :- 10/10

      League of Legends (LoL) is a multiplayer online battle arena video game. It was released in October2009 and   is free-to-play with optional character customization. In League's main mode, Summoner's Rift,a team wins by   pushing through to the enemy base and destroying their "Nexus".

      Available for Platforms :- Windows,Mac



  •  StarCraft

      Developed by :- Blizzard Entertainment, nStigate Games, Swingin' Ape Studios, Saffire
      Published by :- Blizzard Entertainment, Nintendo
      IGN Ratings :- 9.4/10

      StarCraft is a 1998 military science fiction real-time strategy game. The game spawned the StarCraft   franchise, and became the first game of the video game series. It is set in a fictitious future timeline   during the 25th century AD in a distant galaxy known as the Koprulu Sector.

      Available for Platforms :- Windows,Mac,Nintendo



  •  XCOM 1 & 2

      Developed by :- 2K Games, Firaxis Games, MicroProse
      Published by :- 2K Games, Take-Two Interactive, MicroProse
      IGN Ratings :- 8.8/10

      X-COM is a science fiction video game franchise featuring an elite international organization tasked with   countering alien invasions of Earth. The series began with the strategy video game X-COM: UFO Defense in   1994. A reboot series was published by 2K Games, beginning with XCOM: Enemy Unknown in 2012.

      Available for Platforms :- Windows,Playstation
