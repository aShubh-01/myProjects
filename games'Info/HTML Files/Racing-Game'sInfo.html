Racing games may be based on anything from real-world racing leagues to fantastical settings.
They are distributed along a spectrum between more realistic racing simulations and more fantastical arcade-style racing games.
Kart racing games emerged in the 1990s as a popular sub-genre of the latter.






  •  Asphalt Series

      Developed by :- GameLoft
      Published by :- GameLoft
      IGN Ratings :- 9.5/10

      Asphalt is a series of racing video games developed and published by Gameloft. The first game, Asphalt Urban   GT, was released for the Nintendo DS and N-Gage in 2004. A number of spinoffs were also released,such as the   endless runner Asphalt Overdrive.

      Available for Platforms :- Windows,Playstation,Xbox,Mac,Android,IOS,Nintendo


  •  Real Racing Series

      Developed by :- Firemonkey,Firemint
      Published by :- Electroics Arts(EA)
      IGN Ratings :- 9.1/10

      Real Racing is a 2009 racing game developed and published by Firemint for iOS. It was released on June 8,   2009 for iPhone and iPod Touch, and later a HD version was released for the iPad, which featured improved   graphics to take full advantage of the iPad's capabilities. The game was a critical and commercial success,   and has led to two sequels; Real Racing 2 in 2010 and Real Racing 3 in 2013.

      Available for Platforms :- IOS,Android


  •  Hill Climb Racing 1 & 2

      Developed by :- FingerSoft
      Published by :- FingerSoft
      IGN Ratings :- 9.2/10

      Hill Climb Racing is a 2D physics-based racing video game. It was originally created by Toni Fingerroos,   Fingersoft's founder, and is the company's best-known product. The player controls a driver across hilly   terrains, collecting coins along the way.

      Available for Platforms :- Windows,Android,IOS


  •  Forza Series

      Developed by :- Playground Games, Turn 10 Studios, Xbox Game Studios, Sumo Digital, Boss Game Studios
      Published by :- Microsoft Corporation, Xbox Game Studios, Microsoft Studios
      IGN Ratings :- 8.6/10

      Forza is a series of simulation racing games for Xbox consoles, Microsoft Windows, iOS and Android devices.   The series seeks to emulate the performance and handling characteristics of many real-life production,   modified and racing cars. It is primarily divided into two ongoing titles, Forza Motorsport and Forza   Horizon.

      Available for Platforms :- Windows,Xbox



  •  Need for Speed Series

      Developed by :- Electronics Arts(EA)
      Published by :- Electronics Arts(EA)
      IGN Ratings :- 9.8/10

      The series is one of EA's oldest franchises not under their EA Sports brand. The most recent game, Need for   Speed: Hot Pursuit Remastered, was released on November 6, 2020. A free-to-play mobile installment released   in 2015 was actively developed by Firemonkeys Studios.

      Available for Platforms :- Windows,Playstation,Xbox
