Puzzle video games make up a broad genre of video games that emphasize puzzle solving.
The types of puzzles can test problem-solving skills, including logic, pattern recognition, sequence solving, spatial recognition, and word completion.






  •  Portals 1 & 2

      Developed by :- Valve-Halflife Studios
      Published by :- Valve Corporation
      IGN Ratings :- 9.8/10

      Portal is a 2007 puzzle-platform game developed and published by Valve. It was released for Windows,Xbox 360   and PlayStation 3, and has been ported to Mac OS X, Linux, Android (via Nvidia Shield) and Nintendo Switch.   The game's unique physics allow kinetic energy to be retained through portals.

      Available for Platforms :- Windows,Playstation,Xbox,Mac,Nintendo,Android,Linux



  •  Mini-Metro

      Developed by :- Dinosaur Polo Club
      Published by :- Dinosaur Polo Club,Playdigious
      IGN Ratings :- 8.6/10

      Players are tasked with constructing an efficient rail transit network for a rapidly growing city. Levels   are based on real cities and the appearance of stations and passengers is procedurally generated. The game   uses a procedural audio system to generate sounds based on the player actions and transit network.

      Available for Platforms :- Windows,Playstation,Xbox,IOS,Mac,Nintendo,Android



  •  Baba is You

      Developed by :- Hempuli
      Published by :- Hempuli
      IGN Ratings :- 8.4/10

      Baba Is You is a puzzle video game created by Finnish indie developer Arvi Teikari. The game centers around   the manipulation of "rules" to allow the player character, usually the titular Baba, to reach a specified   goal. A free update titled "Baba Make Level" was released on November 17, 2021.

      Available for Platforms :- Windows,Linux,,Mac,Android,IOS,Nintendo



  •  Keep Talking and Nobody Explodes

      Developed by :- Steel Crate Games
      Published by :- Steel Crate Games
      IGN Ratings :- 8.6/10

      Keep Talking and Nobody Explode is a puzzle video game. It was designed around virtual reality support,with   availability first on Android-driven Samsung Gear VR. The game tasks a player with disarming procedurally   generated bombs with the assistance of other players who are reading a manual.

      Available for Platforms :- Windows,Mac,Linux,Xbox,Android,IOS,Playstation



  •  Hitman: Go

      Developed by :- Square Enix Montreal
      Published by :- Square Enix
      IGN Ratings :- 7.5/10

      Hitman Go is a turn-based puzzle video game. Announced in February 2014, the game was released for iOS in   April 2014 and for Android in June 2014. In February 2016, a "Definitive Edition" version was released on   PlayStation 4, PlayStation Vita, Linux and Windows via Steam.

      Available for Platforms :- Windows,Android,IOS
